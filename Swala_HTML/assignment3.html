Kigali, Rwanda


Kigali is the capital city of Rwanda and is known for its cleanliness and saftey. The city is well organised with good roads and green spaces. Kigali is also an important business and cultural centre in East Africa.

  • President: Paul Kagame
  • Population: 13M
  • Continent: Africa
  • Currency: Rwandan Franc

Cairo, Egypt


Cairo is the capital city of Egypt and one of the largest cities in Africa. It is famous for its rich history, ancient pyramids, and the River Nile. Cairo is an important centre for education, tourism, and trade in North Africa

  • President: Abdel Fattah el-Sisi
  • Population: 109M
  • Continent: Africa
  • Currency: Egyptian Pound

Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya and is known as the "Green City in the Sun". it is a major hub for business, technology and international organisations. Nairobi is unique because it has a national park located close to the city centre.

  • President: William Ruto
  • Population: 55M
  • Continent: Africa
  • Currency: Kenyan Shilling
